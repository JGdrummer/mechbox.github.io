About

Mechbox creates fun toys for children to teach them the basics of engineering. We create a variety of toys that you can order online or through stores. Our goal is to teach children and nurture them to the path of engineering. We do not make these products only for children, but also for adults.

Our goal at Mechbox is to enhance children's brain power and nurture their mathematical and analytical skills at a young age in a very fun way. Our products are made from environmentally friendly materials. We value true worksmanship in our company, ensuring your products come with hardwork and care.

Our company is based in Bayanihan, Gapan City, Nueva ecija. Our company has three main brnaches. Manila, Baguio, and in Nueva Ecija.
